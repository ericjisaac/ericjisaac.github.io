About

Hello! My name is Eric, and I'm a software developer with extensive experience in serious games (education, visualization, simulation). I'm also the creator of Publizoid LLC, a game development endeavor focused on creating games that are both accessible and have a positive impact on those who chose to play them. By this, I specifically mean games that are engaging without being addicting, and games that encourage and develop beneficial mental habits and skills.

Professionally, I've worked across a variety of industries including scientific research, educational games, and architectural design technology. I have a solid base of technical skills, which has allowed me to adapt to a wide variety of endeavors. When I’m not developing software, I can often be found oil painting, playing saxophone, or taking long walks in nature.
